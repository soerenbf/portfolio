In the spring of 2015 a project course "Innovation Project" led me to the exiting opportunity of doing a design project in coorporation with car manufacturer, Volkswagen. It was a group project with a time-span of 7 weeks during which several prototypes were developed, both low fidelity and high(er) fidelity concluding with a presentation of the concept and prototype at the Volkswagen headquarters in Wolfsburg, Germany.

During the course I took on the role for the entire class as project manager, leading to extra tasks such as communication through teaching staff with the contacts we had in Volkswagen.

As the everyone who attended the course agreed to sign a non disclosure agreement, I cannot go into details with the specific prototypes developed, nor can I disclose the results.
